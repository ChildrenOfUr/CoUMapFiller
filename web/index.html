Welcome to the Children of Ur Street Decorator!

The Children of Ur Map Filler is a tool to place entities (things that you can interact with in the game) onto streets. All of the items in the lists to your right are entities that can be placed onto the map.

To choose a location to edit you can do one of two thinigs:
1. The easiest thing is to click the
Random Street
button at the top and you will be taken to a street that needs work to be done automatically. You can then navigate to adjacent streets via sign posts or click the
Random Street
button again when you are finished.

OR

2. Go to the Glitch Locations page and type in the location you want to get to. Copy the code from the URL (it's the last part, between the last and second-to-last slashes) and paste it into the box at the top right labeled
Enter TSID to Open
(the code is a TSID), then click the
Load
button. Now you can place any of the items to the right onto the street.

But first, you need to move your player's camera. Somewhere on the screen, you'll find a window titled "Street Preview" with a picture of the street and a list of entities. Every entity in the list is an entity that belongs on the street. Entities listed multiple times need to be placed one per listing. See the blue square (
) on the street picture? That's you. Click anywhere on the picture to go there. You can also walk just like you would in the game with the arrow keys, but gravity doesn't apply. Walk up and down too! If you want the window out of your way, click and drag on the title to move it, or click the arrow () to banish it to the bottom right of your screen. Don't worry, it's temporary banishment, you can bring it back the same way.

Once you see in the picture that you are near where something should be, click on it in the entities panel to pick it up. Then just click on the street (not the window) where you want to put it. You can always move it later. We suggest you check the at the top right. This will show you where players walk, so you will know where to place things on the ground. A tiny bit above the line works best to have the player walk in front of it. Use the arrows that appear under it to flip, rotate, or delete it. You can also delete any item with a dotted border (even one that's picked up) by pressing the Delete key. When you've placed an item from the entities list in the window, you'll notice that it gets crossed off and turned red. That's to keep track of what's already been taken care of.

When you're done placing entities, or just want to take a break, click the
Save Entities
button at the top right. That will save your changes to the server, so you, or anyone else, can load them with the street. As soon as you save the entities, they will be live in the game the next time the street is loaded.

FAQ

Q: What is a "Fox Stop" and how do I place it?
A:This is where a fox stops for brushing. Place a fox instead.

Q: Quoins/Coins aren't labeled. Which ones do I place?
A: Use your best judgement. Place quoins in good spots, but make sure to place the number of quoins that are listed in the street window. Coin and Quoin are the same thing. You can choose which type to place.

Q: The trees listed don't match the ones in the picture. Which do I use?
A: This is because trees are player-changeable, with poison/over-harvesting and bean planting. Use the trees listed in the spots marked in the picture so that everything is crossed off and the street is marked as completed.

Q: What's a spawner?
A: These are for quests. Don't worry 'bout it.

Q: How do I place a sloth?
A: We couldn't find the spritesheet for the sloth, so just leave it for now.

Q: What's a knocker?
A: Those are what hang on the sloth trees to call the sloths. As with the previous answer, leave it for now.

Thank you for your time and help putting the game world together. Happy placing!
